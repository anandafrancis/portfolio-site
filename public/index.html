welcome to my virtual home!


If you couldn’t tell by the website name my name is Ananda Francis and I am a third year Data Science student at Northeastern University. For years I used to get frustrated writing and updating my resume. Of course I know to tailor each resume to the specific position I am applying to but beyond that, I felt that resumes always reflected such a small portion of who I am and the value I bring to a team. I created this site to give a comprehensive representation of who I am, what I value and what I’ve done. When dealing with clients, employers, and partner organizations it’s imperative to have a central hub of information and personality so people can really get to know who they will be working with.
