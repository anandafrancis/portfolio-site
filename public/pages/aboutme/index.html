Lauryn Hill is my favorite music artist to date. Her music is transcendental and continues to carry me through the most difficult times of my life. Many love her for her album "Miseducation of Lauryn Hill": easily top 10 (I'd even argue top 5) in HipHop and in R&B, but my favorite work of hers is the live album she recorded: MTV Unplugged 2.0. The raw emotion, spiritual influence, interaction with the audience, critiques on current society and the social systems that compose it and journey to defining self identity establishes the boundless beauty of the album and the resolve and peace that stirs within me any time I listen to it.


Aside from her music I love reggae and dancehall as an entire genre. Growing up as a Jamaican American there wouldn't be a day that goes by where me or one of my family members aren't playing Bob Marley, Sizzla, Beres Hammond, Vybz Kartel, JahCure, and all the other great musicians of the genre.


Honestly, I don't really see myself as a person that "likes" anything; if I like it, I LOVE it. Within a week of me working my restaurant job and I was telling everyone how much I love it there. As soon as I started my major (a field I learned existed a month before I came to campus), I was talking about how much I love my discipline, mind you I was barely a semester in. I went to Puerto Rico for a week and I spent at least a month after talking about how much I loved the atmosphere and culture of the island and wanted to move there soon after graduation. I'll watch a bad tv show simply because I love the characters and want to see where their story lines take them. I post on Instagram everyday because I love laughing and sharing comedy with others. Every conversation I bring up some obscure thing I love. I just love... everything. My default is just to love things. I bumped into one of my few male friends at Northeastern whom I barely even talk to and my first thought after our brief conversation was "Damn I really love this guy." If you ask me what I love, no time in the world is enough for me to sit and list it all.
